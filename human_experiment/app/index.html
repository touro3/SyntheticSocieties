Economic Decision Game

You will play 10 rounds of a simple economic game. Each round you have three options:

  • Work — earn +10 wealth, but stress increases slightly
  • Save / Rest — recover stress (no wealth change)
  • Cooperate — give 5 wealth to a neighbor; they receive 7.5

Start with 50 wealth. Try to balance wealth and well-being.

This takes about 5 minutes. There are no right or wrong answers.
